Core Token Framework

Primary Tokens

The DeSocX ecosystem consists of four primary token types that form the foundation of the platform's economic system.

A

$ANYN (Reserve)

Governance & Utility

The native governance and utility token of DeSocX. $ANYN implements elastic supply mechanics governed by algorithmic rebasing tied to treasury growth. It serves as the reserve currency for the entire ecosystem.

S

$sANYN (Staked)

Yield & Benefits

A derivative token received when users stake $ANYN. It represents a claim on staked $ANYN plus accrued rebase rewards, growing in value relative to $ANYN over time through regular rebases.

W

$wANYN (Wrapped)

External Integration

A wrapped version of $ANYN designed for interoperability with external protocols. When users entangle $wANYN tokens, they gain access to the Looking Glass feature for real-time asset intelligence.

B

$bANYN (Bonded)

Institutional Access

A bonded token issued to institutional investors, representing a time-locked claim on future $ANYN tokens at a discount. Holders gain exclusive access to the Ghost-Ai system for advanced portfolio analysis.

Specialized Tokens & NFTs

In addition to the primary tokens, DeSocX utilizes specialized tokens and NFTs for specific functionalities within the ecosystem.

PE

Particle-Energy

Energy Credits

Tokenized energy credits representing fixed amounts of energy (kWh) that can be traded, staked, or redeemed at values that dynamically adjust to reflect current energy utility costs.

FD

Feynman Diagram Certificate

Entry NFT

Specialized NFTs exclusively mintable during the Day of Emergence event. When staked, they "collapse" into specific particles with unique properties (spin, mass, charge) that determine their utility and value.

M

Memory NFT

AI Training Data

Digital tokens representing personal or AI-generated memories stored on the blockchain. These NFTs can be used to train and enhance automated agents like Ghost-AI with personalized data and strategies.

AS

Anyon-Shell

Biometric Identity Token

A biometrically-secured digital identity credential that processes biometric data locally and uses quantum-resistant cryptography. The Anyon-Shell token enables anonymous yet fully authenticated interactions within the DeSocX ecosystem.

Multi-Treasury Structure

The DeSocX ecosystem implements a hierarchical treasury structure that enables specialized applications while maintaining system-wide stability.

Main Treasury

Foundational treasury backing $ANYN with physical assets and implementing all stability mechanisms.

Sub-Treasury

Agency or sector-specific treasuries like $USAID for international development initiatives.

Micro-Treasury

Region-specific treasuries (e.g., $AFG, $UKR) representing development bonds for specific areas.

Quantum-Treasury

Highly specialized company or project-specific treasuries for targeted investment.

Supply Elasticity Mechanisms

Four integrated mechanisms maintain economic equilibrium within the DeSocX ecosystem:

1 SPIN

Strategic Protocol Investment Nexus: Manages token issuance with algorithmic distribution to staking rewards, platform development, intrinsic value growth, and community liquidity.

2 CONTINUUM-PRICE

Implements dynamic price ceiling algorithms (typically 6x intrinsic value) to prevent market overheating and excessive speculation.

3 ACCELERATOR

Deploys bonus staking rewards during optimal market conditions to incentivize liquidity growth and token value stability.

4 CHARGE

Provides floor price protection through automated market operations, maintaining minimum token value at physical asset backing levels.
